# Tarot Card Images

Upload your 78 Tarot card images to the appropriate subdirectories:

## Directory Structure

- **major-arcana/**: 22 Major Arcana cards (00-the-fool.jpg through 21-the-world.jpg)
- **wands/**: 14 Wands cards (ace-of-wands.jpg through king-of-wands.jpg)
- **cups/**: 14 Cups cards (ace-of-cups.jpg through king-of-cups.jpg)
- **swords/**: 14 Swords cards (ace-of-swords.jpg through king-of-swords.jpg)
- **pentacles/**: 14 Pentacles cards (ace-of-pentacles.jpg through king-of-pentacles.jpg)

## Naming Convention

Use lowercase with hyphens:
- Major Arcana: `00-the-fool.jpg`, `01-the-magician.jpg`, etc.
- Minor Arcana: `ace-of-wands.jpg`, `two-of-wands.jpg`, ..., `king-of-wands.jpg`

## Supported Formats

- .jpg / .jpeg
- .png
- .webp

Images should ideally be in portrait orientation with aspect ratio 2:3 (e.g., 400x600px or 600x900px).
